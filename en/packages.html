Pricing FAQ

Common Questions About Prices & Packages

How much does it cost to finish a 100 sqm apartment in Egypt in 2026?
Finishing a 100 m² apartment starts from EGP 550,000 with the Standard package, EGP 600,000 for Premium, EGP 1,075,000 for Luxury, EGP 1,425,000 for Super Luxury, and EGP 1,700,000+ for Ultra VIP. Use the calculator above for your exact area.
What is the difference between the 5 finishing packages?
They differ in material quality: Standard (EGP 5,500/m²) local materials, Premium (6,000) partially imported, Luxury (10,750) Italian materials, Super Luxury (14,250) premium international materials, Ultra VIP (17,000+) exclusive bespoke materials.
Do the prices include design and engineering supervision?
Yes — all prices include 3D design, execution drawings, full on-site supervision, quality assurance and after-handover service.
Can I pay in installments?
Yes — a flexible stage-based plan: 30% on contract, 30% mid-execution, 30% before handover, 10% after final inspection.
How much are interior design fees in Egypt in 2026?
Design fees are calculated at a base rate of EGP 130/m²: basic 2D+3D packages from EGP 8,000, full packages from EGP 15,000, luxury detailed packages from EGP 25,000. Gulf clients: remote design from SAR 8/m².
